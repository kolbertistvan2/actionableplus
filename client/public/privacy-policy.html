Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2025

Summary: We collect minimal data necessary to provide our AI consulting service. We do not sell your data to third parties. Your conversations are processed by AI models to provide insights, but are not used to train models without your consent.

1. Who We Are

Actionable+ ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered e-commerce consulting platform operated by Actionable Kft., registered in Hungary. Our service helps e-commerce businesses improve their online performance through AI-assisted analysis and recommendations.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password when you register
  • Conversation Data: Messages you send to our AI consultants, including any business information you share
  • Uploaded Files: Documents, images, or other files you upload for analysis
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service, including features used and session duration
  • Browser Data: When using our browser automation features, we may temporarily access web pages on your behalf

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our AI consulting services
  • Process and respond to your queries with relevant recommendations
  • Personalize your experience with memory features (if enabled)
  • Communicate with you about service updates and support
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI Processing

Your conversations are processed by third-party AI models (including Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, and OpenAI GPT) to generate responses. These providers may process your data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

We use API services that do not train on your data by default.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Conversation history: Until you delete it, or 12 months of inactivity
  • Uploaded files: 30 days after upload, unless attached to a conversation
  • Memory data: Until you disable the feature or delete your account

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Secure authentication with encrypted passwords
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and employee training

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@actionableplus.com

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by email or through the service.

11. Contact Us
